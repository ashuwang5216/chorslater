# Error Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy for consistent multilingual translation QA.

## Critical

- Meaning reversal (for example allow vs deny).
- Omission or addition of legal, medical, safety, or policy-critical information.
- Harmful or offensive mistranslation for protected groups or sensitive contexts.
- Numeric, dosage, amount, or deadline errors that can cause real-world harm.

## Major

- Core meaning drift in key clauses.
- Incorrect domain term that changes operational understanding.
- Wrong politeness/register that breaks business or customer expectations.
- Repeated inconsistency in product terminology.

## Minor

- Non-blocking grammar or punctuation issues.
- Slightly awkward phrasing that remains understandable.
- Style inconsistency that does not alter meaning.

## Labeling Pattern

For each issue, provide:

1. Severity: critical, major, or minor.
2. Segment: short quote or line reference.
3. Problem: what is wrong.
4. Fix: corrected target-language segment.
5. Rationale: why the correction is better.
